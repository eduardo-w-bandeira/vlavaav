About

"The Bread of Life" refers to the teachings of Jesus Christ, as found in the Bible. It signifies the spiritual nourishment that comes from faith in Him and is closely linked to the idea of Jesus being essential for eternal life.

Meaning

In the Bible, specifically in the Gospel of John, Jesus declares, "I am the bread of life" (John 6:35). This means that just as bread is essential for physical sustenance, Jesus is essential for spiritual life. Believing in Him brings eternal life and deep fulfillment that goes beyond earthly needs.

Biblical Verses

  • John 6:35: "Then Jesus declared, ‘I am the bread of life. Whoever comes to me will never go hungry, and whoever believes in me will never be thirsty.’"
  • John 6:48-51: "I am the bread of life. Your ancestors ate the manna in the wilderness, yet they died. But here is the bread that comes down from heaven, which anyone may eat and not die."
